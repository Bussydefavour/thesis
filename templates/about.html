About The App

This app is the culmination of my final year thesis, featuring an ensemble machine learning model designed to predict ad clicks. The goal is to enhance the performance of existing models and provide Nigerian business owners with a tool to predict the success of their marketing campaigns better, helping them adopt strategies that maximize ROI.

The model uses the Avazu dataset from Kaggle and extensive exploratory data analysis (EDA) to glean insights. Irrelevant features were dropped, and the remaining categorical variables were converted to numeric values before being fed into the model. We trained several individual models, including Logistic Regression, Decision Tree, Random Forest, and Naive Bayes, and combined them using a soft voting method.

The final model is deployed via Flask and is ready for use.

How to Use the App:

1. Save your dataset as a CSV file with the following features: id, 'site_id', 'site_domain','site_category', 'app_id', 'app_domain', 'app_category', 'banner_pos','device_id','device_ip', 'device_model', 'device_type', 'device_conn_type'.
2. click "Choose File" on the home page to upload your CSV.
3. After uploading, click "Predict" to get predictions for each observation in your dataset.
